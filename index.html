Founded in 1995, KEC has grown from a small engineering college to a premier institution of higher learning. With a sprawling 250-acre campus and state-of-the-art infrastructure, we have been at the forefront of technical education for over three decades.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about KEC

How can I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our website by filling the application form and uploading required documents. Alternatively, you can visit the college campus for offline application.

Is hostel accommodation available?

Yes, we have separate hostels for boys and girls with all modern amenities including Wi-Fi, 24/7 security, mess, and recreational facilities.

What are the scholarship eligibility criteria?

Scholarships are available for meritorious students (95%+ in 12th), sports quota (national/state level), economically weaker sections, and defense personnel children.

What are the placement opportunities at KEC?

KEC has an excellent placement record with 98% placement rate. Top recruiters include TCS, Infosys, Microsoft, Google, Amazon, and many more. Average package is 6.5 LPA.

What are the admission deadlines for 2026-27?

The application deadline for the 2026-27 academic year is August 31, 2026. Counselling sessions will begin in September 2026.

Does the college provide transportation?

Yes, we operate 50+ buses covering all major routes across the city for the convenience of students and faculty.
